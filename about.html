About Us

Professional leasing, maintenance coordination, and resident services for residential rental properties in California and Ohio.

Interstate Realty Management is responsible for leasing, maintenance coordination, and resident services for a portfolio of residential rental properties in California and Ohio.

Our focus is simple: provide well-maintained housing, communicate clearly, and respond promptly when issues arise. We believe successful rental housing starts with professionalism, organization, and mutual respect.

From the application process through tenancy, our goal is to create a straightforward and positive experience for residents while maintaining high standards across the properties we serve.

Our approach

Quality housing. Clear communication. Responsive service.
